About Cycle4Change

Cycle4Change is our first mega event dedicated to promoting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) through the power of cycling. Join us as we inspire a generation to embrace sustainability and healthy living.

Event Highlights

Community Engagement

Connect with like-minded individuals passionate about change.

Fitness and Fun

Enjoy a day of cycling, fitness challenges, and fun activities.

Prizes and Recognition

Compete for exciting prizes and get recognized for your efforts.
